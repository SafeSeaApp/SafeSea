About SafeSea

SafeSea is revolutionizing maritime navigation with an AI-powered app designed to guide boaters and sailors safely across rivers, lakes, seas, and coastal waters. Founded by a passionate team dedicated to saving lives, SafeSea combines intuitive, Google Maps-like navigation with a groundbreaking Distress Alert System (DAS) that connects users to nearby community members in emergencies. Our mission is to make waterways accessible and secure for everyone, from novice boaters to seasoned naval professionals.
