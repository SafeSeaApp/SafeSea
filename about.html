About SafeSea

SafeSea is revolutionizing maritime navigation with an AI-powered app designed to guide both new and experienced boaters safely across rivers, lakes, canals, seas, and coastal waters via real time data updates at your fingertips. Founded by a passionate team dedicated to saving lives, and making boating great again, SafeSea also provides a groundbreaking Distress Alert System (DAS) that connects users to nearby community members in emergencies. Our mission is to make waterways accessible and safer for everyone, from novice boaters to seasoned naval professionals.

Under the leadership of our founder, SafeSea is building a platform that prioritizes user safety and community support. We are creating a tool that not only navigates but also fosters a network of mariners ready to respond when it matters most. Join us in making every journey on the water a safer one
