About SafeSea

Our mission is to empower boaters and sailors to navigate safely with a user-friendly app. SafeSea combines real-time navigation with an innovative Distress Alert System (DAS) that connects users to nearby community members in emergencies.

Features

  • AI driven navigation via real-time data for rivers, lakes, seas, canals, and coastal ways.
  • Real-time hazardous condition alerts.
  • Distress Alert System to notify nearby users in emergencies.
  • User-friendly interface for new and experienced sailors.
  • Community driven reviews and points of interest.
